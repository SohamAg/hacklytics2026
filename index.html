HeartScape

Interactive 3D cardiac visualization · Hacklytics 2026

HeartScape is an interactive 3D cardiac visualization platform built for congenital heart disease analysis. The left view always shows Patient 1 as the anatomical reference. Use Simulate to see how specific CHD conditions typically deform cardiac anatomy, or Upload Scan to load a holdout patient's segmented MRI and run full PCA + condition attribution analysis.

PCA is trained on 54 patients across 33 CHD conditions from the HVSMR-2.0 dataset. Condition attribution uses feature-space proximity to known condition archetypes. Five holdout patients (1, 14, 16, 28, 57) are available as demo inputs.
