HeartScape

Interactive 3D cardiac visualization

HeartScape is an interactive 3D cardiac visualization platform that transforms medical imaging data into dynamic, explorable models of the human heart. Using real scan-derived anatomical structures, the platform allows users to rotate, slice, annotate, and compare healthy and diseased hearts in an intuitive environment.

By visualizing how conditions such as cardiomyopathy, hypertrophy, or structural defects alter cardiac anatomy over time, HeartScape makes complex medical information accessible to clinicians, researchers, students, and patients alike.

Rather than viewing static images, users can explore the heart as a living system — examining structural changes, understanding disease progression, and interacting directly with patient-specific anatomy.
